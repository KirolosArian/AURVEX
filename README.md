## üéØ Executive Summary

**AURVEX** is a cutting-edge, cloud-based management ecosystem specifically engineered for the gold and jewelry industry. It bridges the gap between traditional craftsmanship and modern technology by eliminating manual errors, preventing fraud, and providing actionable business intelligence.

Unlike generic POS systems, AURVEX is built with the specific nuances of gold in mind‚Äîhandling **karat variances**, **precise gram weights (0.001g)**, **making charges**, and **live market fluctuations** in real-time.

## üíé Core Features & Modules

### 1. üí∞ Smart Transaction Engine
A robust Point of Sale (POS) tailored for gold:
*   **Automated Pricing:** Fetches live global gold prices (GoldAPI) and auto-calculates totals based on weight and making charges.
*   **Multi-Currency & Payments:** Supports cash, credit, and installment payments seamlessly.
*   **Digital Invoicing:** Generates professional, branded PDF invoices instantly, sent via email to customers.

### 2. üì¶ Intelligent Inventory with QR
Modernizing stock management:
*   **Unique Identity:** Every ring, necklace, or bracelet gets a unique QR code containing its specific Type, Karat, and Weight.
*   **Instant Scan:** Staff can scan items using any device camera to populate the sales screen instantly‚Äîno manual entry required.
*   **Live Tracking:** Real-time updates on stock levels, reducing shrinkage and "out-of-stock" scenarios.

### 3. ü§ñ AI-Powered Business Intelligence
Turning data into decisions:
*   **Price Forecasting:** Uses machine learning to predict gold price trends for the upcoming week.
*   **Profit Optimization:** Analyzes sales patterns to suggest the best times to buy or sell inventory.
*   **Smart Chatbot:** An integrated assistant to answer staff queries about stock or system functions instantly.

### 4. üìä Financial Command Center
Comprehensive reporting for owners and accountants:
*   **Daily Settlements:** Automated reconciliation of cash drawer vs. system sales.
*   **Profit & Loss:** Detailed breakdown of revenue, gold costs, making charges, and operational expenses.
*   **Expense Tracking:** Manages rent, salaries, utilities, and other shop expenses in one dashboard.

### 5. üë• Customer Relationship Management (CRM)
Building loyalty:
*   **Profiles:** Stores detailed customer history, preferences, and contact info.
*   **Purchase History:** Tracks every gram bought or sold by a customer for loyalty rewards.
*   **Engagement:** Automated notifications for offers or gold price drops.

## üõ°Ô∏è Security & Architecture

AURVEX is built on a **Three-Tier Architecture** ensuring scalability and security.

### üîê Security Measures
*   **ReCAPTCHA v3:** Invisible protection against bots and brute-force attacks.
*   **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC):** Strict separation of duties between **Shop Owners**, **Sales Staff**, and **Accountants**.
*   **Data Encryption:** End-to-end SSL encryption for all financial data.
*   **Audit Logs:** Every action in the system is logged and traceable to a specific user.

### üèóÔ∏è Technical Highlights
*   **PWA Ready:** Functions as a native app on tablets and phones with offline capabilities.
*   **Real-Time Data:** WebSockets for instant price updates without page reloads.
*   **High Performance:** Redis caching implementation for lightning-fast report generation.
